About

Who I am

I'm Abishek P Nair, a Junior Embedded Systems Engineer based in North Paravur, Ernakulam, Kerala. I hold a B.Tech in Electronics & Communications Engineering from Universal Engineering College, Thrissur and currently work at Purpose Technology LLP, Kochi.

My focus is on building complete hardware-software solutions — bridging circuit-level electronics with reliable firmware. I work across the full stack of embedded development: schematic design, PCB layout, microcontroller programming, and IoT system architecture.

I am particularly interested in wireless communication technologies such as LoRa and Wi-Fi mesh networks, and enjoy the challenge of engineering systems especially Research & Development that perform reliably in demanding real-world conditions.
